Our Story

My name is Dana Highfill, and Iâ€™m the owner and primary operator of Float North. I have spent much of my life recovering and healing from the stress and traumas of our modern world and have created a sanctuary for others to do the same. In the right space, you can put your phone on airplane mode, settle deeply into your body, and take the time to listen.

I first floated in a sensory deprivation tank in 2006. I added it to a deeper yoga and meditation practice and in 2011 I floated for the first time. That float took me deeper than my meditation practice ever had, and I left feeling extremely physically and emotionally reset. That first float was the seed that eventually led me to leave my massage career and open Float North.

Float North is a state-of-the-art wellness facility in NE Portland that serves all people who seek to align physically, spiritually, emotionally, and mentally. With years of research, training, education, grant funding, and seven months to build one of the most beautiful float centers in Portland...
